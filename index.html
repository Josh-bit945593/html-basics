THINGS DOGS SHOULD NOT EAT/DRINK

  • Alcohol
  • Avocado
  • Citrus
  • Coconut and Coconut Oil

Dog Cleaning

Dogs should be bathed at least once a month how ever somebreeds require a bath once a week! after every bath your dog should be brushed!

Dog Feeding

Your dog should be fed at least twice a day, unless consulted by a vet not to. Some dogs just have food laying out all the time; however, this can be unhealthy for your dog and may make him/her obese. Depending on the type of dog and what his/her vet says, some dogs should get breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Dogs should eat dog food or some types of people food.

Dog care

Taking care of a dog involves providing proper nutriation , regular exercise,grooming,andveterenary care. its important to understand the specific needs of your dog to keep them healthy and happy

Dog Training

Training your dog is essential for their well-being and helps create a strong bond between you and your pet. Basic commands like sit, stay, and come are fundamental, and positive reinforcement techniques are highly effective.
